Cat Care

  • Feed your cat a balanced diet
  • Feed your cat a balanced diet appropriate for their age, size, and breed. Consult with your veterinarian to determine the best food and feeding schedule for your cat.
  • Provide fresh water at all times
  • Make sure your cat always has access to clean, fresh water.
  • Provide a clean litter box
  • Make sure your cat's litter box is clean and in a quiet, private location.
  • Provide a scratching post
  • Cats need to scratch to keep their claws healthy. Provide a scratching post or pad for your cat to use.
  • Provide toys and playtime
  • Cats need mental and physical stimulation to stay healthy and happy. Provide toys and playtime for your cat.
  • Regular vet check-ups
  • Regular veterinary care is essential to keep your cat healthy. Make sure your cat gets regular check-ups and vaccinations.
  • Keep your cat indoors
  • Keep your cat indoors to protect them from dangers such as cars, predators, and diseases.
